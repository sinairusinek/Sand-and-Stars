About the Project

This digital edition was created as part of the project "Hybrid, annotated publication of the novel manuscript 'Sand and Stars' by Siddy Wronsky and accompanying contemporary historical documents" of the German Central Institute for Social Issues (DZI).

The digital edition and the eBook (https://doi.org/10.5771/9783748940814, Nomos Verlag) were created during the project period from August 2022 to December 2023. It contributes to research on exiled social workers in the League of Nations Mandate for Palestine and their significance for the development and academization of social work in Israel.

The project team consists of:

  • Dr. Yehudit Avnir (The Paul Baerwald School of Social Work and Social Welfare, Hebrew University Jerusalem)
  • Dr. Ayana Halpern (The Paul Baerwald School of Social Work and Social Welfare, Hebrew University Jerusalem)
  • Dr. Dayana Lau (Alice Salomon Archive at the Alice Salomon Hochschule Berlin)
  • Dr. Stephanie Pigorsch (DZI, Berlin)
  • Burkhard Wilke (DZI, Berlin)

Cooperation partners are the Central Zionist Archives (CZA) in Jerusalem, where the original manuscript can be found under file number A149/10, and the e-Lijah-Lab, a laboratory for digital humanities at the Department of Jewish History and Biblical Studies at the University of Haifa (Dr. Sinai Rusinek and Dr. Eliezer Baumgarten).

The project was realized thanks to the funding programme for the digitization of cultural assets of the State of Berlin supported by the Berlin Senate Department for Culture and Social Cohesion, implemented by the Research and Competence Center Digitization, digiS, at the Zuse Institute Berlin.
